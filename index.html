My Story

The chapters that made me who I am.

2008 – 2016

🌱 Childhood in Cumilla

Growing up in Brahmanpara, surrounded by green fields and the warmth of family. Simple days filled with play, wonder, and the magic of a small-town childhood.

2016 – 2023

📖 School Life

From Uttar Chandla Ideal School to Chandla K.B High School and then Cumilla Police Lines High School — each place shaped me, taught me, and helped me grow into who I am.

2023 – Present

🌍 Life in Dhaka

Moved to Dhaka for Dhaka Imperial College. A new city, new challenges, new dreams. Learning to be independent while staying true to myself.

The Future ✨

🌙 InshAllah

I dream of a life that's peaceful and meaningful. Creating things that matter, traveling, and living a life my younger self would be proud of. The best is yet to come, InshAllah.
